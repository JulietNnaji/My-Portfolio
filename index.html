Juliet Nnaji

Multi-passionate creative from Lagos, Nigeria.

Welcome to my page! My name is Juliet Nnaji, I am a remarkable spoken-word poet who does poetry about social isues affecting people especially women. I believe there are many stories people try to blanket in a smile; and in a world where women are still trying to find their voice, i try to be a mouthpiece to nudge them on.
